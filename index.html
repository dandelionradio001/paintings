Is Paintings free to use?

Yes. You can browse the collection, view artworks, and build your favorites right after installing.

Why won't some images load?

Artwork images are streamed from the web, so a stable internet connection is required. If an image doesn't appear, check your connection and pull down to refresh.

Can I save artworks to my device?

Yes. Open any artwork, tap to view it full-screen, and use the download button to save it to your Photos.

How do I switch between Light and Dark mode?

Go to the “My” tab → Appearance, and choose Light, Dark, or System to match your device.
